**verbascore** is an AI-powered call analytics platform that automatically analyzes every inbound and outbound call, scores sales reps, and connects marketing spend directly to closed deals.

## key features

* **100% call analysis** — automatically transcribes and scores every call with 95%+ accuracy
* **real-time rep coaching** — personalized feedback on quickness, introduction, knowledge, hospitality, and call to action
* **revenue tracking** — connects ad spend to closed deals so you know exactly which campaigns drive revenue
* **custom scoring** — tailor evaluation criteria to your specific business needs
* **seamless integration** — works with existing VoIP, cloud phone, and call tracking systems
* **agency support** — manage multiple client accounts from a single dashboard

## results

* +37% close rate improvement
* -64% lead wastage reduction
* setup in under 24 hours

SOC 2 compliant, HIPAA ready, end-to-end encryption.
