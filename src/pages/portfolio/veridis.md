**veridis** is a cryptographic verification layer for real world product claims. brands and suppliers generate cryptographic proof for claims like organic, locally sourced, or certified — instead of relying on PDFs and trust.

## key features

* **zero knowledge verification** — suppliers prove claims while keeping pricing, formulas, volumes, and customer lists private
* **AI-powered data ingestion** — automatically extracts and normalizes data from invoices, certificates, and spec sheets
* **on-chain proof anchoring** — verified claims become timestamped and immutable on blockchain
* **universal API** — integrates with DPP platforms, ERP systems, and compliance tools
* **supplier-first design** — single verification accepted across all brands and platforms

## use cases

targets HORECA (restaurants/hospitality), textiles/fashion, and EU ESPR-regulated products requiring Digital Product Passports.
